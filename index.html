About Me

Design and support, built around how you actually work.

I'm Jeff A. Bazar, currently a 3rd year Computer Engineering student at Dr. Emilio B. Espinosa Sr. Memorial State College of Agriculture and Technology. Outside the classroom, I work as a Virtual Assistant and freelancer under my own brand, JB Design, helping entrepreneurs, startups, and businesses stay organized, productive, and visually impactful.

My technical background, combined with my creativity and attention to detail, lets me provide reliable support so clients can focus on growing their business while I take care of the essentials behind the scenes — from designing brand assets and product interfaces, to building the front-end that brings them to life, to keeping business data accurate, organized, and easy to access.

As a lifelong learner, I continuously improve my skills by exploring new design trends, digital tools, and technologies. My goal is to build long-term relationships with clients through dependable support, creative solutions, and excellent service.
